## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q1: How long does deletion take?
**A**: Account deactivation happens within 24-48 hours. Complete data deletion from all servers takes up to 30 days.

### Q2: Can I recover my account after deletion?
**A**: No. Deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.

### Q3: What if I have active subscriptions?
**A**: Currently, the app is free. If you have any paid features in the future, cancel them before requesting deletion.

### Q4: Will my customers be notified?
**A**: No. We do not contact your customers or vendors.

### Q5: Can I create a new account with the same email later?
**A**: Yes, after 30 days of complete deletion, you can create a new account with the same email.

### Q6: What about GST records?
**A**: For legal compliance, we may retain minimal GST transaction records (anonymized) as required by Indian tax laws. All personal identifiers are removed.

### Q7: Can I just delete my business data but keep the account?
**A**: Yes! Just email us with this specific request instead.

### Q8: What if I signed up with Google?
**A**: The deletion process is the same. We'll disconnect your Google account and delete all data.

## Data We Cannot Delete

- Anonymized GST transaction records (as required by Indian tax law)
- Aggregated analytics (no personal information)
- Essential backup logs for 30 days
