# Privacy Policy for Billing Management App

**Last Updated: November 6, 2025**

---

## Introduction

Welcome to **Billing Management** ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the application.

---

## Information We Collect

### 1. Personal Information

When you register for an account, we collect:
- **Full Name**
- **Email Address**
- **Password** (encrypted and securely stored)
- **Google Account Information** (if you choose Google Sign-In)

### 2. Business Information

To provide GST-compliant invoicing services, we collect:
- Business Name
- GSTIN (Goods and Services Tax Identification Number)
- Business Address
- Phone Number
- Email Address
- Business Logo (optional)

### 3. Financial Information (OPTIONAL)

**You can choose to skip this information**. We collect financial details only if you provide them:
- Bank Account Details (Name, Number, IFSC Code, Branch)
- UPI ID
- Payment Records

**Important**: All financial information is **optional** and can be added, edited, or removed at any time from the Business Details page.

### 4. Customer and Vendor Information

To help you manage your business, we collect:
- Customer/Vendor Names
- Contact Details (Phone, Email)
- Addresses
- GSTIN (if applicable)

### 5. Transaction Data

We store your business records:
- Invoices and Bills
- Product/Service Information
- Payment Status
- Due Dates
- Tax Calculations

### 6. Files and Media

If you choose to upload:
- Business Logos (PNG/JPG)
- Digital Signatures
- Receipt Photos (optional)

### 7. Device Information

We automatically collect:
- Device Type and Model
- Operating System Version
- App Version
- Crash Reports and Diagnostics

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

1. **Provide Core Services**
   - Generate GST-compliant invoices and bills
   - Calculate taxes automatically
   - Manage customer and vendor records
   - Track inventory and stock

2. **Account Management**
   - Create and manage your account
   - Authenticate users securely
   - Sync data across devices

3. **App Improvement**
   - Analyze app performance
   - Fix bugs and crashes
   - Improve user experience

4. **Customer Support**
   - Respond to your inquiries
   - Provide technical assistance
   - Send important updates about the service

## Data Storage and Security

### Encryption
- **In Transit**: All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS
- **At Rest**: All data is encrypted and stored securely on Firebase (Google Cloud)

### Security Measures
- Industry-standard encryption protocols
- Secure authentication with Firebase Auth
- Regular security audits
- Restricted database access
- Automatic backups

### Data Location
Your data is stored on Firebase Cloud Services (Google Cloud Platform) servers, which may be located in various regions globally.

1. **Access Your Data**
   - View all information stored in the app

2. **Edit Your Information**
   - Update business details anytime
