### Step 1: Create New Page
1. Go to your Google Site

### Step 2: Add Content
1. Copy content from `PRIVACY_POLICY_WEBSITE.md`
2. In Google Sites, click "Text box"
3. Paste the content
4. Format as follows:

### Formatting Tips:

**Main Title** (Privacy Policy for Billing Management App)
- Format: Heading 1 (H1)
- Align: Center
- Font: Large

- Format: Normal text

- Align: Center

## Design Recommendations

### Colors (Based on App Theme)
- **Primary**: #2196F3 (Blue)
- **Accent**: #FFC107 (Amber)
- **Background**: #FFFFFF (White)
- **Text**: #212121 (Dark Gray)

### Fonts
- **Headings**: Roboto Bold or Open Sans Bold
- **Body**: Roboto Regular or Open Sans Regular
- **Size**: 16px for body text (readable)

### Layout
- **Max Width**: 800-900px for readability
- **Margins**: Good white space around content
- **Line Height**: 1.6 for comfortable reading
